Research
SceneCut: Semi-automatic segmentation, reconstruction and separation of 3D objects
Gemmechu Hassena, Jonathan Moon, Ryan Fujii, Andrew Yuen, Noah Snavely, Steve Marschner, Bharath Hariharan
[coming soon]

How can we separate objects within a scene without a provided segmentation mask? Given point clicks in a single view, SceneCut will generate a mask, propagate it to other views, reconstruct the 3D surface, and separate the objects in the scene.
Quantifying Bird Skeletons
Zhizhuo Zhou , Gemmechu Hassena, Brian C. Weeks, David F. Fouhey
CV4Animals Workshop, CVPR 2021
[PDF] [Bibtex]

We introduced a dataset and system for measuring skeletal traits from museum specimens, which speeds up skeleton measurement by 15x. This research helped Ecologists in their study of how global warming causes the size of birds to shrink
